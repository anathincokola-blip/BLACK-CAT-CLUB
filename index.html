About Us

Who We Are

BLACK CAT CLUB is a football club based in Nomlacu, Bizana. The club exists to build young football talent and give boys a positive, disciplined and supportive environment.

We focus on football development, teamwork, respect, fitness, commitment and community pride.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop young players into disciplined footballers who can represent their community with pride.

We help players grow through training, match preparation, mentoring, development contracts and strong team leadership.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become one of the respected community football clubs in Bizana by creating opportunities for young players.

Club Values

  • Discipline
  • Teamwork
  • Respect
  • Hard work
  • Development
  • Community support
